Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act Funding: 2021 – 2023

The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, or Bipartisian Infrastructure Law, or BIL as it is clunkily know, was passed into law in November 2021.

The bill included $550 billion in new funding for transportation and roads, broadband access, clean water, and electric grid renewal.

This map looks at projects that have received, or been announced to receive, funding across the country and across all government agencies.

Methods and data sources

Data pulled from multiple sources, cleaned & queried in QGIS, and then combined on the fly in the browser.

Funding data provided by: General Services Administration

Population Data via: US Census Bureau
